Layman overview:

The proposed project aims to improve agricultural insurance by using blockchain and its immutability. It employs blockchain and smart contracts to create a secure, unchangeable record of farm data and insurance transactions. Real-time environmental data, such as temperature, humidity, soil moisture, and rainfall, is collected through sensors and weather APIs and stored on a blockchain platform for accuracy and easy access.

A simple mobile app developed entirely using Kotlin and Jetpack compose gives farmers access to this data, helping them monitor conditions, understand risks, and make better insurance decisions. Smart contracts streamline the insurance verification process, reducing errors and speeding up claims processing, resulting in quicker payouts and increased customer satisfaction. Blockchain technology ensures transparency and reduces the risk of fraud, building trust between farmers and insurance companies. The project aims to enhance transparency, efficiency, and resilience in agricultural insurance, driving positive changes in the sector.
